# Germany-Safe Development Rules

Status: short internal rule set, 2026-06-28. Not legal advice.

- Do not process more data than needed.
- Do not import everything by default.
- Do not store third-party passwords.
- Do not store tokens without server-side encryption, rotation and deletion.
- Do not log sensitive data, tokens, prompts, full receipt text, raw files, search queries or connector payloads.
- Do not analyze Private Vault documents automatically.
- Do not send unnecessary data to AI providers.
- Do not enable a real AI provider before DPA/AVV, region, retention, no-training/data-use and deletion are reviewed.
- Do not add tracking, analytics, pixels or newsletter tooling without consent/legal review.
- Do not hide affiliate links or referral behavior.
- Do not expose private uploaded files through public/static URLs.
- Do not claim "fully GDPR compliant", "legally safe", "100% secure" or guaranteed warranty/legal/insurance outcomes.
- Do not ship public launch without German privacy lawyer or external DSB review.
- Keep provider inventory, processing map, cookie audit, Supabase review and risk register current whenever data flows change.
